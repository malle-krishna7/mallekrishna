AI X-Ray Diagnosis

An AI-assisted concept to support faster screening and clearer insights from X-ray images.

PythonAIWeb

Overview

This project explores a workflow where users upload an X-ray, the system runs analysis, and the UI presents a simple summary plus confidence indicators. The focus is on clarity and safety.

Notes

  • Designed for explainable outputs
  • Clear disclaimers + guidance
  • Simple, fast UI

What I Focused On

UX

Make results understandable and avoid “black box” screens.

Pipeline

Clean flow from image upload to analysis and response rendering.

Safety

Communicate limitations clearly (assistive, not definitive).
